About Us

Kavi   Travelers

Kavi Travelers was recognised as one of the best travel companies for holidays in Sri Lanka long before the island became one of the hottest holiday destinations in the world. For 20 years, our dedicated and talented representatives in the UK and Sri Lanka have tailored memorable holidays to our clients' exact requirements. Our own Colombo-based company, Kavi Travelers, ensures we can provide an expert personal service and unbeatable value for money so there is no point struggling on alone. As well as a constantly-assessed choice of accommodation to suit all budgets, we also offer a wide selection of specialist itineraries, a huge and enticing range of activities and we regard our pool of chauffeur-guides as the best in the country.
